Facial Emotion Recognition

A Convolutional Neural Network is used to classify an image into one of the seven emotional states namely - angry, disgusted, fearful, happy, neutral, sad and surprised. VGG16 pre-trained model is used as the base network and is fine-tuned for this problem. The model takes images of dimension (50, 50, 3) and the output is one of the seven emotion classes.
